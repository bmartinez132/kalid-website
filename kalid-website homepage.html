All About Us

Hello, we are Kalib. Kalib is a group name, our members are Bridgette Martinez, Kylie Chow, and Karen Cazares. We got together in a group project for Girls Who Code. Our goal here is to create a creative outlet for teens! BY TEENS FOR TEENS!



Our Purpose

Mental illness is something that is close to all of our hearts since so much stigma surrounds those who have mental illnesses. We want you all to express yourself in a safe environment where others can hear you out and support you.



HOTLINES

Don't be afraid, we're here to help.
