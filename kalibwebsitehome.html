PURPOSE

KALIB is a site that wants to help those who are suffering from a mental illness. Even if you do not have one, anyone is welcome to come and relax. We live in a world where we cannot avoid stress. It is hard to remember that we need to make time for us to take care of ourselves. Our mental health is just as important as our physical health. Everyone has a different level of stress tolerance and stress, and we would like to cater to everyone's need. Enjoy your time at KALIB!
