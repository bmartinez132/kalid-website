What is Mental Health?

Mental health is a person's emotional and psychological well - being. It is important to keep our minds happy and healthy. In order to keep ourselves in check lookout for signs that could mean you are exhibiting a type of mental illness.

Signs and Symptoms

- Irregular eating

- Isolation

- Feeling like nothing matters

- Aches and pains

- Feeling helpless

- Smoking, drinking, using drugs more than normal

- Feeling confused and/or a wide range of emotions

- Fighting with others

- Having mood swings

- Thoughts and memories that persist in your head

- Hearing voices or believing things that are not true

- Thinking of self - harm or harming others

- Unable to perform daily tasks
