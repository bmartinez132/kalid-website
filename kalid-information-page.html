Mental health is a person's emotional and psychological well - being. It is important to keep our minds happy and healthy. In order to keep ourselves in check lookout for signs that could mean you are exhibiting a type of mental illness.

Signs and Symptoms - Irregular eating - Isolation - Feeling like nothing matters - Aches and pains - Feeling helpless - Smoking, drinking, using drugs more than normal - Feeling confused and/or a wide range of emotions - Fighting with others - Having mood swings - Thoughts and memories that persist in your head - Hearing voices or believing things that are not true - Thinking of self - harm or harming others - Unable to perform daily tasks

Types of Mental Illnesses

Anxiety Disorder: Overwhelming anxiety that becomes repetitive.

Autism (Autism Spectrum Disorder): Developmental disorder that makes it arduous to socialize and communicate with others.
